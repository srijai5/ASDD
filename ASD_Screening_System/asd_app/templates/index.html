MultiSync ASD

Bridging Technology and Care with Multimodal Intelligence

🌟 What is MultiSync?

MultiSync ASD is a pioneering screening tool that uses "Multimodal Fusion" technology. By looking at both physical traits and behavioral patterns simultaneously, it provides a more comprehensive developmental snapshot than traditional paper-based tests.

🧠 How It Works

Our tool analyzes specific facial biomarkers using computer vision while processing your observations from the AQ-10 survey. This "dual-sync" method ensures that the physiological and behavioral markers are cross-referenced for higher accuracy.

🤝 Our Mission

We believe in early intervention. This tool is designed to be a supportive first step for parents, helping you gather objective data to share with your pediatrician, leading to faster support for your child's unique journey.
